
1. Boil pennes till they are al dente
2. Make béchamel according to your favourite instruction
3. Make red sauce from onions, meat and passata. Add cinnamon, pepper, salt according to taste.
4. On a baking tray assemble three layers: first pennes, then red sauce, then béchamel, sprinkle some cheese on top. 
5. Bake in 180C approx. 30-45 min still the top is golden brown
6. Wait 20-30 miin for the food to cool down. Being a hipster and eating before it was cool is not recommended.
